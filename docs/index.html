Daily Power Outage Data by Region in Ukraine

Updated on 04.02.2026

Prepared by the Data & Modelling team from Green Deal Ukraïna: Robert Carr & Dr. Frank Meissner
Email contact - robert.carr [at] helmholtz-berlin.de
Check out our other dashboard that focuses more generally on the state of the Ukrainian energy system.

This interactive webpage shows the scheduled and actual hours of power outages reported by households in Ukraine each day across 15 different administrative regions (oblasts). Data collection is commissioned by the Green Deal Ukraïna project and collected daily via a survey. Each daily value per region is the average of two sub-queues of consumers. Consumers in Ukraine are divided into 12 sub-queues per region, each with a roughly equal electricity demand. Scheduled outages are intended by the authorities to be equally distributed across sub-queues within a region. As we add new data, more dates will become available from the drop down list below.

About the project

Green Deal Ukraïna (GDU) is a German, Ukrainian, and Polish project that aims to support Ukraine in making future-proof energy and climate policy decisions. It is funded by the German Ministry of Research, Technology, and Space (BMFTR). The three main work streams are data & modelling, policy advice, and capacity building. In addition, GDU contributes to the development of the newly established Kyiv Energy and Climate Lab in Ukraine.
